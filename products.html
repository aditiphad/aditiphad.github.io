Poha papad is dry product famous in the western coasts of Konkan.It is basicaly prepared using pressed rice that is "Poha" known in Marathi.Its spicy flavour adds to a wonderful taste and can be eaten as a starter.

Sandgi mirchi is a Konkan special recipe. Green chillies are stuffed with spices and dried under hot sun. These taste just awesome. This is an age old traditional recipe. This is a perfect side dish and once prepared can be used throughout the year.

This crisp and spicy papad is made from Nachni. They are rich in antioxidants, fibres and nutrients which are vital for human body.Nutritional benefits of Nachni: Nachni has a high content of protein.It is an extremely good source of iron and calcium.

Spicy, tangy, and loaded with flavor, Green Chilli Pickle or Hari Mirch ka Achar is a delicious Indian style pickle recipe where fresh green chilies are pickled with mustard seeds and lemon juice. This pickle goes very well with any Indian meals.

Raw mango or tender mango is variety of fruit used for pickling. There are multiple varieties of mango pickles prepared depending on the region and the spices used but broadly there are two types: whole baby mango pickles and cut mango pickle. Whole baby mango pickle is a traditional variety very popular in Southern India and uses baby mangoes that are few weeks old.

It is an ideal taste enhancer condiment that can be shared with the choice of parathas or with any rice variant. there are myriad ways to make lemon-based pickle and this is an instant version of it.
